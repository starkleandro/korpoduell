Korpoduell – Data Deletion Request

Last updated: 29.11.2025

If you would like to delete your account or request deletion of any personal data stored by Korpoduell, please follow the steps below.

How to request data deletion

Send an email to:

leandrostark@corpoduell.com

with the subject:

“Korpoduell Data Deletion Request”

In the message, include:

Your username

The email address used for your account (if applicable)

Confirmation that you want your data deleted

What data will be deleted

Upon request, we will delete:

Your account

Login information

IP logs associated with your account

Match data

Game statistics and progress

How long it takes

Data deletion is usually completed within 7 days.
You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been removed.

Contact

If you have any questions, contact:

leandrostark@corpoduell.com
