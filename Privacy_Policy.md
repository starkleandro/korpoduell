Privacy Policy for Korpoduell

Last updated: 29.11.2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Korpoduell (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile game.

By using Korpoduell, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

a) Account Information

When you register an account, we collect:

Username

Password (stored securely and never in plain text)

Email (if provided)

b) IP Address

When you create an account or log in, we temporarily store your IP address for:

Security and anti-abuse protection

Preventing fraudulent activity

Account verification measures

We do not use IP addresses for tracking outside of security purposes.

c) Gameplay and Match Data

During gameplay, we collect:

Match results

Player actions (correct/incorrect answers, timing)

Stats and progression

Scores and ranking info

This data is required to operate in-game features such as matchmaking, leaderboards, and game history.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data only for the following purposes:

To create and manage your account

To provide gameplay functionality (matchmaking, results, stats)

To maintain security and prevent cheating or abuse

To operate and improve the game

We do not:

Sell your data

Share your data with third parties

Use your data for advertising or tracking outside the app

All data is stored on our own server and is only accessible by the developer.

3. Data Storage and Security

We take appropriate technical measures to protect your data, including:

Secure server infrastructure

Encrypted password storage

Restricted access (only the developer can access stored data)

No system is perfectly secure, but we aim to keep your information protected to the best of our ability.

4. Children’s Privacy

Korpoduell is not intended for children under 13.
If we learn that a minor has registered without permission, we will remove the account.
